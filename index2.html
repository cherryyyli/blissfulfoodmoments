Skewer Kitchen

The Perfect place for supper in Hong Kong

Been here 2 times a week, they serve the best Japanese BBQ, you must try the signature Pork lollipop, lychee pork and the black sugar pineapple!

Aji MGM Cotai Macau

Unforgettable Japanese A4 beef Ramen

The beef melts into your mouth instantly, blend the egg yolk with ramen, it could be the best comfort food in winter.

Bluebottle Wanchai

The best WFH cafe you could ask for!

The cafe has 3 floors, it is spacious and filled with natural sunlight, making me more motivated to work. Also, you should try their mushroom sandwich, it is cheesy with a hint of sourness, making it fresh and appetizing. For coffee, the Latte portion is bigger than Cappuccino.

Pistol Lounge CWB

Get your customized cocktail after work!

Located on the 26th floor in the Sharp, knock on their door and wait to be seated for a magical journey with your friends.

Cotiz Macau

All I Need Is a Pistachio Pancake for Tea

The thick flavor Pistachio ice cream, best matched with the souffle pancake. Pistachio Lovers, if you happen to be around in Macau, you must go visit Cotiz, they serve the best puff and the environment is Instagrammable and chill for catching up with friends!

Sushiro

The best valued sushi train in Hong Kong

My favorite sushi place to go to, the sushi is good value and has a lot of choices, they also release seasonal food and dessert, making it appealing to keep going back.

Ching Ching Dessert

Super Localized Hong Kong Sweet soup in Wanchai

The mix of traditional and innovative Hong Kong sweet soup, the must-try dessert is custom-made by the boss, the "Rice ball with Rose Wine Fillings in Ginger soup", it is warm and surprising.

SSAL.BORI.SSAL

Calling Korean Food Lovers! You must visit this Korean Food street in TST!

It is one of the most popular shop, luckily we got in early like around 6 pm, we saw a lot of people lining up outside. Fried Rice cake, Fried chicken, and Kimchi Soup are must-try!
